This is the story of our 2011 Kayak in BC in the discovery islands group. Full gallery is [here](https://lmblevins.smugmug.com/Travel/2011-BCKayak).  This was getting pretty long, so I did separate posts for the base camps describing [Solstua West](/blog/2011-bc-kayak-solstua-west) and [Go With the Flow](/blog/2011-bc-kayak).

Sunday
------

We had flown into Vancouver the night before. On Sunday,  we took a small 30 passenger plane from Vancouver south terminal to Campbell River. Took a taxi to the ferry, ferry to Quadra Island, then taxi to our B&B - [Harbour House](http://www.harbourhouse.bc.ca/). Harbour House is owned by a nice woman named Susan, with a beautiful view over Harriott Bay. Met Diane from Nanaimo, who would be going on the trip with us.

Bought some wine and cheese and had dinner on the deck of the B&B. Afterwards we went for a little walk, nice sunset - we're on an island, so the picture is facing east.

Wednesday
---------

Wednesday we moved camps from Solstua West on Rendezvous to Go With the Flow on Maurelle island. We packed all of our stuff in the Kayaks. Since we weren't actually "camping", we had room to spare in the big sea kayaks. The crossing from Rendezvous to Read island was a little rough, but pretty fun - you definitely felt like you were not in a lake!

<p>
{% fig "IMG_1025", "https://photos.smugmug.com/Travel/2011-BCKayak/i-C4pGgLg/0/LV6xFDgQ9XTNd5vKTHDDXpjmbDmsvMrdXLsrfnMf9/M/IMG_1025-M.jpg", "https://photos.smugmug.com/Travel/2011-BCKayak/i-C4pGgLg/0/K2bbnCHDwhfSgxBBbZvR7j8nFdcpnQDXQJGPc7qXF/O/IMG_1025.jpg", 450, 600, "On the move" %}

We stopped here at Surge Narrows, which is pretty much the central "town" for all of the islands. No store, no police, and the only floating post office in Canada.

<p>
{% fig "IMG_1052", "https://photos.smugmug.com/Travel/2011-BCKayak/i-kr76fQ8/0/LkzsPBhHFfWDfcDS693Lr7tcQ7MPbqnmvFxNhcjH7/M/IMG_1052-M.jpg", "https://photos.smugmug.com/Travel/2011-BCKayak/i-kr76fQ8/0/Kc9DCrk5sm7jhVqVXXH5ZztKb2VBgTZPDXFKttsqm/O/IMG_1052.jpg", 450, 600, "Floating Post Office" %}

The Surge Narrows elementary school and community center. The kids in the islands have to boat here until the sixth grade. After that, they either go to Campbell River or do correspondence.

{% fig "IMG_1048", "https://photos.smugmug.com/Travel/2011-BCKayak/i-n9jHWM3/0/MFXh8xRGC7vPbt8828zRhDCn9KbTC9fVDkBWZFvDr/M/IMG_1048-M.jpg", "https://photos.smugmug.com/Travel/2011-BCKayak/i-n9jHWM3/0/NLNkDHPfZtV92WqFrjVmS5SpBtxWnDX8Z9PJhqMbq/O/IMG_1048.jpg", 450, 600, "School" %}
