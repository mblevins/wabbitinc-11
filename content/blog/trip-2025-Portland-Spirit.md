At the end of September, 2025, we took a cruise from Portland to Astoria on the [Portland Spirit](https://www.portlandspirit.com). 
Full gallery is [here](https://lmblevins.smugmug.com/Travel/2025-Portland-Spirit)

We went the night before, with a drink at a local bar, some browsing at Powell's, and a dinner at [Dolly Olive](https://www.sesamecollective.com/dollyolive). Portland was a war zone, and we barely escaped with our lives.
